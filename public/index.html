Frequently Asked Questions

What teaching methods do you use to help children learn to read?

We combine phonics, sight words, and interactive storytelling to build strong decoding skills and comprehension in a fun, engaging way.

How do you keep young learners motivated in writing?

Through creative prompts, games, and hands-on activities, we make writing enjoyable while gradually developing grammar and sentence structure.

What’s the class size, and is it personalized?

We keep groups small (max 6-8 students) to ensure individualized attention and tailored support for each child’s pace.

How do you assess progress?

We use informal checks like reading fluency observations and writing samples and share regular updates with parents.

My child struggles with focus how do you handle short attention spans?

We use quick, engaging activities to keep lessons dynamic and match young learners' energy levels.
